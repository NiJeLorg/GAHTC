Current Members

GAHTC membership is based upon the principle of community and collaboration. We currently have over 200 members from all over the world, of various ranks and disciplines. Of those members, 68 are contributing members, with content in the GAHTC library. Over the next three years, the GAHTC's aim is to provide opportunity, through its various grant options, for members to share their research and knowledge with the GAHTC community, and our GAHTC community. As membership continues to grow,, so will our community, and our network. Join GAHTC and receive access to some of the best architectural history survey courses covering a diverse set of topics and regions. GAHTC members are eligible to apply for research funding, travel funding, and workshop funding, as well as funded attendance to the Member's Conference to collaborate, interact and innovate with fellow global scholars. Questions?

Contact GAHTC Project Manager, Eliana Hamdi Murchie at emurchie@mit.edu or click here to apply for membership.

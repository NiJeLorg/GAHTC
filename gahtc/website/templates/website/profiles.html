Current Members

The GAHTC is managed by a project manager and a core team of board members. Together they endeavor to extend the reach of architectural survey with the aim of enhancing the global knowledge of architectural history.

The GAHTC was co-founded by board members Mark Jarzombek (MIT) and Vikram Prakash (University of Washington) who continue to be heavily involved in advancing the collaborative's Mission, and in extending its global pedagogical reaches. Project Manager Eliana Hamdi Murchie and Designer Andre Malan are responsible for the everyday operation of the collaborative.

In addition, the GAHTC is supported by a team of board members; Robert Cowherd (Wentworth Institute), Carla Keyvanian (Auburn University), Gail Fenske (Roger Williams University), Suzanne Marchand (Louisiana State University), and Adnan Morshed (The Catholic University of America).

Bi-Annual Members' Conference

Phase 2 of the GAHTC will include two members' conferences, the first in fall 2017, and the second in fall 2019. The Members' Conference is a forum for content presentations, teacher to teacher workshops on pedagogical techniques, as well as opportunity to collectively discuss GAHTC goals, progress, and imperatives.

The GAHTC will fund travel, lodging, and meals for all members attending the conference.
